State Machine AI implementation for a Boss Enemy.

Boss functionality

The boss enemy is driven by a state machine with 5 phases based on its health.

*Phase 1 uses a static attack and a dynamic position-based attack.

*Phase 3 adds a new static attack and projectile attacks.

*Phase 5 combines all previous attacks in sequence.

*Phases 2 and 4 are defensive, where the boss becomes invulnerable and spawns enemies.

Extra functionalities

Other techniques I implemented for this AI

*Attack Pool for static and dynamic attacks.

*Projectile Pool for releasing projectiles.

*Intro & Outro Cut Scenes
